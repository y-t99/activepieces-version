## Authentication

1. Follow the [link](https://discord.com/developers/applications) provided.
2. In the "Name" field, enter a name for the application.
3. Check the necessary checkboxes.
4. Click the "Create" button.
5. Go to the "OAuth2 > General" page.
6. Copy the "Client ID" and save it for later use.
7. Reset the "Client secret" to obtain the initial secret and save it.
8. Click the "Add Redirect" button and enter the "OAuth Redirect URL from Activepieces" in the "Redirect" field. Save these changes.
9. Go to the "Bot" page.
10. Click the "Add Bot" button and confirm the warning.
11. Reset the "Token" to obtain the initial bot token and save it.
12. In Activepieces, enter the "Client ID", "Client Secret", and "Bot Token" values in their respective fields.
13. Click the "Submit" button. You can now use your new Discord connection within your flows.
